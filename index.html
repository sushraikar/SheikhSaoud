About Sheikh Saoud

A visionary leader dedicated to excellence and global collaboration

H.H. Sheikh Saoud bin Faisal Al Qassimi is a distinguished leader with over 25 years of experience in business, investments, and global collaboration. Educated at Ajman University with a background in banking from the United Arab Bank, Sheikh Saoud has built an impressive career spanning multiple industries and international organizations.

As a UN Peace Messenger since 2016, Sheikh Saoud has dedicated himself to promoting peace, understanding, and sustainable development worldwide. His leadership roles include Secretary-General of ICAFE, Advisory Board Member of WAFUNIF, and Chairman of multiple successful enterprises including Marvellex Group and SBF Establishment.
